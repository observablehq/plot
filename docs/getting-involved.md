# Getting involved

Excited about Plot, and want to help? Great! We’d love you to join us.

Please star the [observablehq/plot](https://github.com/observablehq/plot) repo on GitHub to help get the word out.

Visit GitHub and upvote [the features](https://github.com/observablehq/plot/issues) you want the most. If you don’t see your feature there, file an issue and tell us what you want!

Sign-up for Observable and share your work.

Sign-up for the Observable Plot Twist newsletter.

Follow Observable on Twitter.

Follow Observable on Observable.

Visit the Observable forum and help the community by answering questions.

Join the Observable Slack to meet other people that are interested in Plot, visualization, and analysis.

Want to open a pull request to Plot? Read our [guide to contributing](https://github.com/observablehq/plot/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md).
